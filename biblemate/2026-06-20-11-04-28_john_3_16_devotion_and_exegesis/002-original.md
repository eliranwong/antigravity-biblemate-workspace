# Step 002: Original & Interlinear Greek Text (John 3:16)

**Persona**: Oxford Bible Scholar  
**Reference**: John 3:16  
**Versions**: Open Hebrew Greek Bible (OHGB), Open Hebrew Greek Bible Interlinear (OHGBi)

---

## 1. Greek Text (OHGB)

> **John 3:16 (OHGB)**  
> Οὕτως γὰρ ἠγάπησεν ὁ Θεὸς τὸν κόσμον ὥστε τὸν Υἱὸν τὸν μονογενῆ ἔδωκεν ἵνα πᾶς ὁ πιστεύων εἰς αὐτὸν μὴ ἀπόληται ἀλλ᾽ ἔχῃ ζωὴν αἰώνιον

---

## 2. Interlinear Text (OHGBI)

> **John 3:16 (OHGBI)**  
> Οὕτως (Thus) γὰρ (for) ἠγάπησεν (loved) ὁ (-) Θεὸς (God) τὸν (the) κόσμον (world), ὥστε (that) τὸν (the) Υἱὸν (Son), τὸν (the) μονογενῆ (only begotten), ἔδωκεν (He gave), ἵνα (so that) πᾶς (everyone) ὁ (-) πιστεύων (believing) εἰς (in) αὐτὸν (Him) μὴ (not) ἀπόληται (should perish), ἀλλ᾽ (but) ἔχῃ (should have) ζωὴν (life) αἰώνιον (eternal).

---

## 3. Word-by-Word Linguistic Mapping

| Greek Word | Transliteration | English Gloss | Function / Significance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Οὕτως** | *Houtōs* | Thus, in this manner | Adverb of manner; sets the pattern of God's love. |
| **γὰρ** | *gar* | for | Explanatory conjunction linking to the context of verses 14–15. |
| **ἠγάπησεν** | *ēgapēsen* | loved | Aorist active indicative of *agapaō*; denotes completed, decisive historical action. |
| **ὁ Θεὸς** | *ho Theos* | God | Subject; the supreme source and initiator of redemptive love. |
| **τὸν κόσμον** | *ton kosmon* | the world | Direct object of God's love; humanity in rebellion against God. |
| **ὥστε** | *hōste* | that, with the result that | Conjunction indicating result; introduces the consequence of God's love. |
| **τὸν Υἱὸν** | *ton Huion* | the Son | Object given; Christ's distinct personhood as Son of God. |
| **τὸν μονογενῆ** | *ton monogenē* | the unique / only begotten | Adjective modifying the Son; unique in kind and relationship. |
| **ἔδωκεν** | *edōken* | He gave | Aorist active indicative of *didōmi*; the action of sacrifice/delivery. |
| **ἵνα** | *hina* | so that, in order that | Conjunction introducing purpose/result clause. |
| **πᾶς** | *pas* | all, everyone | Universal adjective; highlights the scope of the invitation. |
| **ὁ πιστεύων** | *ho pisteuōn* | the one believing | Present active participle; ongoing, persistent trusting faith. |
| **εἰς αὐτὸν** | *eis auton* | into/in Him | Object of belief; union with Christ. |
| **μὴ** | *mē* | not | Negative particle of condition or potential. |
| **ἀπόληται** | *apolētai* | should perish | Aorist middle subjunctive of *apollymi*; destruction, loss of eternal destiny. |
| **ἀλλ᾽** | *all'* | but | Strong adversative conjunction contrasting perishing with life. |
| **ἔχῃ** | *echē* | should have / possess | Present active subjunctive of *echō*; ongoing possession of life. |
| **ζωὴν** | *zōēn* | life | Accusative singular noun; the gift of salvation. |
| **αἰώνιον** | *aiōnion* | eternal | Adjective modifying life; quality and duration of the age to come. |
