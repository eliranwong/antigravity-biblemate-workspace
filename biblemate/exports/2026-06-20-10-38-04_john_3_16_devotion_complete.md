### Key Word Analysis for John 3:16

1. **ἠγάπησεν (agapaō - G25)**: Divine, sacrificial love based on choice and character rather than mere emotion. It is active and gives.
2. **μονογενῆ (monogenēs - G3439)**: Unique, only-begotten, or one-of-a-kind. Highlights the unique relationship between the Father and the Son.
3. **πιστεύων (pisteuō - G4100)**: Trusting, believing, placing faith in. Not merely intellectual assent but personal reliance on Christ.
4. **ζωὴν αἰώνιον (zōē aiōnios - G2222, G166)**: Eternal, everlasting life. Life of the age to come, starting now and continuing forever in fellowship with God.

### Devotional: The Matchless Love of God

God's love is not a passive sentiment; it is a powerful, active force that went to the cross of Calvary. He gave His most precious, unique Son so that you and I might not perish but have everlasting life. It calls for a response: will you believe? Will you place your trust in Him today?

#### Pastoral Prayer
Heavenly Father, I thank You for Your unspeakable gift on the cross. I turn from my sin and put my faith in Jesus Christ, my Lord and Savior. Amen.

<!-- Source: 005-application.md -->

### Practical Life Application

1. **Active Giving**: Mirror God's sacrificial love by actively giving of our time, resources, and love to those around us, especially the needy.
2. **Heart Renewal**: Regularly examine our hearts to ensure our trust rests fully in Christ and not our own efforts or achievements.
3. **Sharing the Gospel**: Boldly share this simple, life-saving message of God's love and the gift of Jesus Christ with friends, family, and neighbors.
