# BibleMate Study: Joshua 3 - Beyond the Beginning Sermon Study

## Original User Request
Perform a deep study of Joshua 3 and write a sermon titled 'Beyond the Beginning' based on requirements in temp.md

## Refined User Request
Perform an extensive, scholarly, and pastoral study of Joshua 3 (crossing of the Jordan) to produce a publication-quality expository sermon titled **"Beyond the Beginning"**. The sermon must contain three distinct, building, actionable points with deep exegesis, historical context, illustrations, and theological depth. It should speak to mature Christians while providing concrete guidance for young people. The sermon must align with the style of Billy Graham (evangelistic, cross-centered, repentance, faith) and Bill Johnson (presence-centered, supernatural risk, signs, and wonders) without naming them. Additionally, generate small-group discussion questions, scriptural prayers, a devotional, and raw study steps.
